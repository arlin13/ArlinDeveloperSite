Hobbies


To travel

I love to travel. It always amazes me how different life is in different places of the world.

I really love knowing new places and new people, I enjoy speaking with the locals to know their culture, their perspective of the world

Another thing that I love of travelling is that it challenges you in different ways.

From moving in a city / country / continent that you don't know, to try food for the first time, speak in a foreign language or even be in a place where you don't know their language, so you have to communicate with signs and gestures!
